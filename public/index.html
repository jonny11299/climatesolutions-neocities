Climate Solutions

This website is to outline clear action steps that one can take to care for the Earth.

Angst --> Positive Action.

The goal is to take a complex problem (climate change) and break it into actionable steps, so that people can have fun engaging with nature, building projects, creating positive outcomes, and feeling like a part of an important cause.

Problems range from "Simple lifestyle change" to "Sustained manual labor outdoors" to "Help the Earth from your laptop" to "Hey, imagine this climate-friendly business idea."

Check out the Solutions page to explore exciting ways to help the Earth heal.

This website is largely a blog + home for the ideas that fly through my noggin.

I intend to dedicate my life to developing solutions to help the Earth, our communities, and nature heal. I want to pass on a world to our descendents that's worthwhile and full of love, life, and purpose.
